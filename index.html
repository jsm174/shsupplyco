About S&H Supply

Family-owned and proudly serving the community since 1988

S&H Supply specializes in building materials, tools, lawn, garden, paint, millwork, flooring, cabinetry, electrical, plumbing, heating, and more. Additionally, we offer a showroom and kitchen designing services to match. We are also an Interstate battery dealer for all your vehicle battery needs. If you need equipment we rent machines from jackhammers to excavators and offer repairs on some equipment. As your local True Value hardware store we feature popular products, competitive pricing, knowledgeable experts and top-notch customer service. We take pride in serving you, so call us and let us know how can we help you today.
